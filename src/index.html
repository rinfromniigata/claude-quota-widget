How to locate your sessionKey

  1. Log in to claude.ai in your browser.
  2. Right-click on the page and select Inspect to open Developer Tools.
  3. Navigate to the Application tab (Chrome/Edge/Safari) or Storage tab (Firefox).
  4. Expand Cookies in the left menu and select https://claude.ai.
  5. Find the cookie named sessionKey and copy its entire value.
  6. Paste the key into the input field above, name it, and click Add.
